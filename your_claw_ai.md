## 📌 Context for the AI
This repository, **Global TrustDrive 7.0**, is a "Trust-as-a-Service" platform built for the **Visionary AI: NURIE.AI 2026 Cross-Platform Innovation Awards**. It utilizes the **VaultSage Server-side API** to automate cross-border credential authentication.

### 🏆 Competition Integration
- **VaultSage Usage:** The app uses VaultSage for file upload, AI processing, jurisdictional analysis, and the SmartDrop™ sharing ecosystem.
- **Goal:** Provide a seamless, AI-native experience for students and professionals to verify documents for overseas study/work in 9+ countries.

### 🧠 AI Instructions for this Project
1. **Security First:** Never expose `VAULTSAGE_API_KEY`. Use `.env` files for local development.
2. **Standardization:** All document analysis must map to international standards (e.g., EDU-WRL-2026 for Study, JOB-OB-3.0 for Employment).
3. **Multi-Language:** Maintain the English, Traditional Chinese (zh-TW), and Korean (ko-KR) translations in `frontend/src/i18n/`.
4. **Validation:** Ensure that the "Gap Advisor" logic correctly identifies missing documents based on the destination country's official requirements.
